Clean layout

Don't fill yout page up with stuff. White space is good - it enhances the look of your site. Use fonts that will be available on the all computers so your page doesn't end up looking messy.

Navigation

Site navigation plays a huge role in determining how long visitors stay and explore your site. If you have an easy to use navigation system, you'll keep your visitors around longer.

Organisation

The way you organise your content, is as important as what your page looks like, so spend time on it. If your readers can't find what they are looking for, they'll just go look someplace else.

Readable text

Make sure your text is big enough to read. I know, you're probaly thinking 'thank you captain obvious.'Well, i hate to state the obvious, but i am amazed at the number of sites I go to that i have to squint to read. Please, don't punish me for actually wanting to read your content.

Indentity

Ensure your site has a brand indentity that reflects your brand values. Try to avoid cliche stok imagery.

Contrast

It's hard to read dark text on a dark background and light tect on a light background

Keep learning

Stay up to date with news by reading desing blogs or try learning a new web language. There is no reason to stop learning.

Don't give up. Sometimes your ideas won't work, but just keep swimming. Today people tendt to quit or give up too easily. With a little hard work, you'll find most of your ideas......are actually possible.
